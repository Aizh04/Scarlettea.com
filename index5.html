Any time is Milktea time!

Bubble tea is a beverage that has gained huge popularity in many destinations around the world. You can purchase kits and ingredients to make your own at home, and you can also purchase bubble tea from many cafes and eateries.

These drinks come in the form of fruity drinks or milk teas that can be served chilled or hot depending on your choice. They are served with small tapioca balls added to them, which are often known as pearls or boba.
